# Bank Loan Policy Document

This document describes the loan products, eligibility policies, credit score
guidelines, and required documents used by the Intelligent Loan Eligibility
Assistant. All monetary amounts are in Indian Rupees (₹).

---

## 1. Personal Loan Policy

- **Purpose:** Unsecured loan for personal needs (medical, travel, education, etc.).
- **Minimum age:** 21 years.
- **Maximum age at loan maturity:** 60 years.
- **Minimum monthly salary:** ₹30,000.
- **Minimum credit score:** 700.
- **Maximum loan amount:** Up to 10 times the applicant's monthly salary.
- **Loan tenure:** 12 to 60 months.
- **Interest rate:** 11% to 18% per annum, based on credit profile.

## 2. Home Loan Policy

- **Purpose:** Secured loan for purchase or construction of residential property.
- **Minimum age:** 21 years.
- **Maximum age at loan maturity:** 65 years.
- **Minimum monthly salary:** ₹40,000.
- **Minimum credit score:** 725 (a score of 750+ is preferred for the best rates).
- **Maximum loan amount:** Up to 60 times the applicant's monthly salary, subject
  to a maximum of 80% of the property value.
- **Loan tenure:** Up to 30 years.
- **Interest rate:** 8.5% to 11% per annum, based on credit profile.

---

## 3. Credit Score Guidelines

| Credit Score Range | Rating      | Impact on Loan Decision                          |
| ------------------ | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| 750 - 900          | Excellent   | Eligible for best interest rates.                |
| 700 - 749          | Good        | Eligible; standard interest rates apply.         |
| 650 - 699          | Fair        | May be rejected or offered a reduced amount.     |
| Below 650          | Poor        | Loan application is typically rejected.          |

- The minimum credit score to be considered eligible for any loan is **700**.
- Applicants below the threshold are encouraged to improve their score and reapply.

---

## 4. General Eligibility Rules

An applicant is considered eligible for a standard loan when **all** of the
following criteria are satisfied:

- **Age:** 21 years or older.
- **Monthly salary:** ₹30,000 or higher.
- **Credit score:** 700 or higher.
- **Requested loan amount:** Not more than 10 times the applicant's monthly salary.

If any criterion is not met, the application is rejected and the applicant is
provided with the reason(s) and next steps.

---

## 5. Required Documents

Applicants must submit the following documents:

1. **Identity proof:** Aadhaar card, PAN card, or passport.
2. **Address proof:** Utility bill, rental agreement, or passport.
3. **Income proof:**
   - Salaried: Last 3 months' salary slips and last 6 months' bank statements.
   - Self-employed: Last 2 years' income tax returns (ITR) and audited financials.
4. **Employment proof:** Employment certificate or appointment letter.
5. **Photographs:** Two recent passport-size photographs.

---

## 6. Next Steps for Applicants

- **If eligible:** Submit the required documents for verification, after which the
  loan is sanctioned and disbursed within 3 to 7 working days.
- **If not eligible:** Review the rejection reasons, improve the relevant criteria
  (for example, raise the credit score above 700 or reduce existing liabilities),
  and reapply after 3 months.

---

## 7. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the minimum salary required for a personal loan?**
A: The minimum monthly salary required for a personal loan is ₹30,000.

**Q: What credit score is needed for a home loan?**
A: A minimum credit score of 725 is required for a home loan; 750 or above is
preferred for the best interest rates.

**Q: What documents are required?**
A: Identity proof, address proof, income proof, employment proof, and two recent
passport-size photographs. See Section 5 for details.

**Q: How can an applicant improve eligibility?**
A: Improve the credit score above 700, reduce existing liabilities, maintain a
stable income, and reapply after 3 months.
